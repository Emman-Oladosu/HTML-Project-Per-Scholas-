Chocolate Fudge

Description

Chocolate fudge is a rich and creamy dessert that melts in your mouth. Itâ€™s perfect for chocolate lovers and easy to make at home.

This recipe requires only a few ingredients and can be prepared in under 30 minutes, making it a great treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate chips
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate chips and butter together.
  2. Stir in condensed milk and vanilla.
  3. Pour mixture into a pan and let it cool.
